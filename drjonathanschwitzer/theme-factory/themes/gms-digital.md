# GMS Digital

A bold, data-driven theme inspired by Growth Marketing Studios — a Miami-based AI-powered marketing agency. Combines deep midnight navy with electric blue accents to convey authority, innovation, and measurable results.

## Color Palette

- **Midnight Navy**: `#020381` - Deep authoritative base, backgrounds and hero sections
- **Electric Blue**: `#0693e3` - Primary accent, CTAs and interactive elements (vivid cyan blue)
- **Bright Indigo**: `#2874fc` - Secondary accent, gradients and highlights
- **Pure White**: `#ffffff` - Clean backgrounds, body text on dark surfaces
- **Vivid Purple**: `#9b51e0` - AI/innovation accents, feature callouts
- **Charcoal**: `#32373c` - Body text on light backgrounds, UI elements

## Gradients

- **Signature gradient**: `rgb(2, 3, 129)` → `rgb(40, 116, 252)` — Use for hero sections, banners, and section dividers
- **Accent gradient**: `#0693e3` → `#9b51e0` — Use for feature highlights and AI-themed elements

## Typography

- **Headers**: Montserrat Bold
- **Body Text**: Open Sans

## Spacing & Shadows

- Block gap: 24px
- Natural shadow: `6px 6px 9px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.2)`
- Deep shadow: `12px 12px 50px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.4)`

## Best Used For

Digital marketing agencies, healthcare marketing, medical practices, AI/automation companies, Miami-based brands, growth-focused tech businesses, performance marketing decks and reports.
